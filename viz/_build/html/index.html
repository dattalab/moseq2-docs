Welcome to the moseq2-viz documentation¶

Just like the other MoSeq-related packages our lab developed, moseq2-viz provides two methods of interaction: the command-line interface (CLI) or the application-programming interface (API). Most users will stick with the CLI, but if your needs require a more custom approach to ask a scientific question, check out the API.
